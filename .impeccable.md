# MOVVE Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Mixed audience:
- **Fortune 500 supply chain managers**: Executives making high-stakes logistics decisions with accountability and precision needs
- **SME business owners**: Companies needing reliable shipping solutions with budget constraints and growth ambitions
- **Individual consumers**: People tracking personal shipments, comparing services, expecting transparency
- **Logistics professionals**: Industry specialists seeking technical details and capabilities

### Brand Personality
Premium logistics - sophisticated, established, trustworthy, enterprise-grade confidence. The brand should feel like working with a partner who understands high-stakes operations and delivers with precision and professionalism.

### Aesthetic Direction
Bold authority - commanding presence, data-driven, impactful, confident. The visual language should communicate scale, capability, and market leadership without feeling aggressive or overwhelming. Think: "We know what we're doing, and you can trust us with your most critical shipments."

### Design Principles

1. **Confidence through clarity**: Every element should convey expertise and reliability through clear, decisive design choices
2. **Data-driven aesthetics**: Use data visualization, metrics, and operational insights as visual elements that demonstrate capability
3. **Enterprise-grade minimalism**: Clean, refined surfaces that feel sophisticated and established, not cutting-edge or experimental
4. **Progressive disclosure**: Show power users the depth they need, while keeping the surface accessible for newcomers
5. **Trust through transparency**: Make capabilities, status, and processes visible to build confidence in operations

## Technical Context

### Current Stack
- React 18 + TypeScript 5.6
- Vite build system
- Tailwind CSS for styling
- Framer Motion for animations
- Vite for deployment to Vercel

### Target Experience
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Dark mode primary (professional, data-heavy context)
- Performance optimized (critical for enterprise)
- Accessibility first (WCAG AAA compliance)
- PWA capabilities (field use context)
